About Nadafile

nadafile is a simple way to keep info safe on a USB drive, work computer, or mobile device.

I (dandavis) wrote it to keep a backup copy of my contacts on a thumb drive apart from my phone, without doxing my frinds and family were the drive lost or stolen.

Running inside an html file, no software or drivers are required to unlock and view or info on any web browser. Each save creates a new copy, allowing easy archiving+revisions and multi-platform compatibility without installation.

The encryption is AES-265 GCM, an industry standard authenticated cipher used by modern browsers and governments alike. nadafile uses a slow complicated process to turn the password into the encryption key to prevent brute-forcing. As it's self contained and needs no perms or install, it's easier to cleanup and remove without leaving a trace than traditional applications or apps.

More info and the code is available on the github project page
